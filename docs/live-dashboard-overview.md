# Live Dashboard in SaberTask

A complete overview of the SaberTask **Live Dashboard** — the single screen that planners, department heads and admins open first thing in the morning to see exactly what's happening across the company in real time. This document is the source of truth for the **Live Dashboard** page on the SaberTask website.

> **Scope note:** SaberTask has dedicated pages for **Employee Management**, **Task Scheduling**, **Quality Controls**, **Invoicing & Billing** and **Customer Portal**. This page describes the live operational view only — the features for managing the underlying data live on those other pages.

> **How to read this document:** Each section starts with a short, marketing-ready description, followed by a feature list. Every feature notes whether it lives on the **web admin** (planners and managers), the **mobile app** (frontline workers), or **both**.

---

## At a glance

The Live Dashboard is the operational heartbeat of SaberTask. Open it and you immediately see today's work, who is currently on the move, what's running late, who's out sick, and what's coming up over the next two weeks — all from one screen, all updated live, no spreadsheets, no toggling between systems.

---

## 1. Today at a glance

The dashboard opens on a three-column layout that summarises today in seconds.

| Panel | What it shows | Where |
|---|---|---|
| **Today's Tasks** | Every task scheduled for today, with three quick tabs: Open, Finished, All. Each row shows the customer, location, service type and current status. Click any row to open the full task detail dialog. | Web |
| **Active Teams** | Every team currently working today, with a live progress bar (completed vs. total tasks) and remaining count. Each team has its own colour for fast visual scanning. | Web |
| **Overdue Tasks** | Anything scheduled before today that hasn't been completed or cancelled — with a clear red date so nothing falls through the cracks. | Web |

---

## 2. Live route tracking

Click any active team and the dashboard expands a live route tracking panel right next to it.

- **One-click team drill-down** — selecting a team in the Active Teams panel opens a route-tracking view inline, without leaving the dashboard. *(Web)*
- **Live GPS position** of the team as they move through the day's stops. *(Web)*
- **Distance driven and completion percentage**, updated as the team's mobile app sends route pings. *(Web)*
- **Pause and resume awareness** — the dashboard knows when a team paused, for how long, and why. *(Web)*
- **Today-only focus** — the panel always shows the current day's session, so managers never have to filter by date. *(Web)*

> The data behind this panel comes from the same route session that field workers start from their mobile app — no extra data entry, no separate tracking tool.

---

## 3. Sick leave at a glance (admin / planner only)

Admins, planners and department heads see a sick leave block at the top of the dashboard so they always know who's out today.

- **Currently sick** — names and start dates of every employee on active sick leave. *(Web)*
- **Direct entry to admin actions** — open the full sick leave management view in one click. *(Web)*

> *Full sick leave registration, history and analytics live on the Employee Management page.*

---

## 4. Two-week activity calendar

Below the panels, the dashboard shows a 14-day calendar that gives a forward-looking view of the operation.

- **14-day forward view** — current and next week, side by side. *(Web)*
- **Per-day status pills** — each day shows the number of tasks in **Scheduled**, **Draft** and **Completed** state at a glance. *(Web)*
- **Today highlight** — the current day is visually highlighted so it's always easy to find. *(Web)*
- **Week navigation** — jump backwards or forwards a week at a time, or snap back to "this week" with one click. *(Web)*
- **ISO week numbers** — every row is labelled with its week number for easy reference in planning conversations. *(Web)*
- **Click any day** to open a popup listing every task on that date — drill all the way down without leaving the dashboard. *(Web)*

---

## 5. Drill-down everywhere

The whole dashboard is designed to take you from "something looks off" to "I've fixed it" in a couple of clicks.

- **Task detail dialogs** — click any task in any panel to see the full task: customer, location, status, history, attachments, signatures, worker notes. *(Web)*
- **Day-detail dialog** — click any day in the calendar for a focused list of every task on that date. *(Web)*
- **Team-detail expansion** — click any team for live route tracking, without losing the rest of the dashboard. *(Web)*
- **"View all" links** — every panel has a quick path into the full task list when you want more than the top items. *(Web)*

---

## 6. Always live, always shared

The dashboard is built to reflect the real state of the business right now.

- **Real-time data** — the dashboard pulls from the same database as the mobile app and the rest of the web app, so what you see is what's true.
- **Multi-role aware** — the layout adjusts to your role. Admins, planners and department heads see the sick leave block; everyone else sees the operational panels.
- **Multilingual** — the entire dashboard respects each user's language preference (English, Danish and more).
- **Mobile companion** — workers see their own view of "today" on the mobile home screen; the Live Dashboard is the manager's counterpart on the web.

---

## What it looks like for each role

**For an admin or operations manager:**
Open the dashboard in the morning and immediately see who's out sick, what tasks are scheduled, which teams are running, who's behind, and what the next two weeks look like.

**For a department head:**
Track your own teams' progress live, jump into a route to see where the crew is right now, and clear overdue work before it piles up.

**For a planner:**
Use the calendar pills to balance the week ahead, click into any day to rebalance tasks, and reach the full task list with one click when something needs deeper attention.

---

## Why teams use the Live Dashboard

- **One screen, whole operation** — no juggling tabs to find out what's going on.
- **Live, not stale** — the data is the same data your workers' phones are writing in real time.
- **Designed for the morning standup** — the layout maps to the questions you actually ask: who's working, who's behind, who's out, and what's coming.
- **From overview to detail in two clicks** — drill from a panel into a task, a team's live route or a day's schedule without losing context.
- **No setup required** — every customer's dashboard is populated automatically from the work already happening in SaberTask.
