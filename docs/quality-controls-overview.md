# Quality Controls in SaberTask

A complete overview of every SaberTask feature for inspection, quality reporting and proof of work — built so frontline workers can capture quality on site, in any language, on any phone, and managers can spot issues before customers do. This document is the source of truth for the **Quality Controls** page on the SaberTask website.

> **Scope note:** SaberTask has dedicated pages for **Live Dashboard**, **Employee Management**, **Task Scheduling**, **Invoicing & Billing** and **Customer Portal**. Anything that belongs there is intentionally not covered here.

> **How to read this document:** Each section starts with a short, marketing-ready description, followed by a feature list. Every feature notes whether it lives on the **web admin** (planners and managers), the **mobile app** (frontline workers), or **both**.

---

## At a glance

Quality Controls in SaberTask gives you customisable inspection templates, mobile-first report filling with photos and signatures, manager review on the web, PDF export for customers, and a live feed of recent issues by location. It replaces clipboards, paper checklists and "we'll email you the report later" with a clean digital trail tied to every job.

---

## 1. Custom report templates

Build the inspection forms your business actually uses — not a one-size-fits-all template.

- **Template builder** — admins design quality report templates from scratch or duplicate existing ones. *(Web)*
- **Sectioned structure** — group questions into logical sections that match how the inspection actually flows. *(Web)*
- **Mixed response types** — text answers, happy/unhappy ratings, image uploads and signature fields in any combination. *(Web)*
- **Reusable across locations** — one template can be used for any number of customer locations. *(Web)*
- **Tenant-scoped** — every company gets its own templates; nothing is shared across customers. *(Web)*
- **Edit and version freely** — update a template and all future reports use the new version. *(Web)*

---

## 2. Mobile report filling

Quality reports are filled on the phone, where the worker is — not back at the office.

- **Quality reports on the home screen** — workers see and start reports right from the mobile app. *(Mobile)*
- **Section-by-section filling** — the report opens with the same structure the admin built. *(Mobile)*
- **Happy / unhappy quick ratings** — capture customer-perceived quality in one tap. *(Mobile)*
- **Photos from the camera or gallery** — attach as many as needed, directly to the section that they belong to. *(Mobile)*
- **Free-text answers** in the worker's preferred language. *(Mobile)*
- **Customer feedback fields** — capture what the customer actually said, on the spot. *(Mobile)*
- **Auto-save as you go** — the report is preserved even if the phone goes offline mid-fill. *(Mobile)*

---

## 3. Signatures & finalisation

Once the inspection is done, signing it off and locking it down is a single step.

- **Customer signature capture** — the customer signs on the phone screen and the signature is attached to the report. *(Mobile)*
- **Worker signature capture** — captures who completed the inspection. *(Mobile)*
- **Re-sign if needed** — wipe a signature and recapture without losing the rest of the report. *(Mobile)*
- **Finalise the report** — locks the data and timestamps the completion. *(Mobile)*
- **Three states** — Draft, Finalised, Archived — so partially-filled reports never accidentally count as complete. *(Both)*

---

## 4. PDF export for customers

Every finalised report becomes a clean, branded PDF — without an extra step.

- **One-tap PDF export** from the mobile app. *(Mobile)*
- **Branded with the tenant logo** — the PDF carries your own company's identity. *(Both)*
- **Sharable** — send the PDF to the customer over email, messaging or any standard share sheet on the phone. *(Mobile)*
- **Includes everything** — sections, answers, photos, ratings and signatures all in one document. *(Mobile)*

---

## 5. Manager review on the web

Quality reports are not just for the frontline — managers see and review them from the desk.

- **Quality reports list** in the web app, scoped to the company. *(Web)*
- **Open and review** any draft, finalised or archived report. *(Web)*
- **View dialog** showing every section, answer, photo and signature exactly as filled. *(Web)*
- **Manager notes** — add private notes that the customer never sees. *(Web)*
- **Status filtering** — narrow to drafts, finalised, archived, or any combination. *(Web)*

---

## 6. Filter, search & spot patterns

Quality data is only useful if you can find what matters.

- **Filter panel** — slice reports by status, date range, customer, location and template. *(Web)*
- **Search by location or customer** — jump straight to the inspection you remember. *(Web)*
- **Date range navigation** — focus on the last week, the last month, or any specific period. *(Web)*

---

## 7. Recent issues feed

The "what's gone wrong this week" view that managers actually use.

- **Recent issues notice** — a feed of the latest unhappy ratings and quality concerns across the company. *(Both)*
- **By location** — see which sites have repeated issues and which are consistently clean. *(Both)*
- **Trend awareness** — recurring issues at the same location surface naturally instead of disappearing into a folder. *(Both)*
- **Click-through to the report** — go from the issue feed straight into the original report in one click. *(Both)*

---

## 8. Tied to the work that produced it

Quality reports are not floating documents — they're attached to the job they relate to.

- **Linked to tasks** — workers can finalise a quality report against the task they just completed. *(Both)*
- **Linked to locations** — every report belongs to a specific customer location for fast retrieval. *(Both)*
- **Visible in the customer portal** — finalised reports show up automatically for the customers you've granted portal access to. *(Both)*
- **Attached to the audit trail** — the worker, the time, the device and the signatures are all recorded. *(Both)*

> *Customer-facing access to quality reports is described on the **Customer Portal** page. The internal task workflow lives on **Task Scheduling**.*

---

## 9. Multilingual & multi-device

Quality should work in every worker's first language.

- **English and Danish out of the box**, with more languages on the roadmap. *(Both)*
- **Per-employee language preference** — every worker fills the report in their own language. *(Both)*
- **Phone, tablet and web** — fill on a phone, review on the web, share as a PDF anywhere. *(Both)*

---

## What it looks like for each role

**For a quality manager or admin:**
Design the inspection templates that match your business, monitor finalised reports across every location, watch the recent issues feed for emerging patterns, and review individual reports with private manager notes.

**For a department head or planner:**
Use the filter panel to find reports by location and date, click through to the original report when something needs follow-up, and stay on top of recurring issues at problem sites.

**For a frontline worker:**
Open the mobile app, fill the inspection while you're on the floor, snap photos, capture happy/unhappy ratings, get the customer's signature, finalise — and the PDF is ready to share before you've left the building.

---

## Why Quality Controls in SaberTask is different

- **Built mobile-first** — the worker fills the report where the work happens, not from a desk later.
- **Customisable templates** — you build the forms your business actually uses, with the response types you need.
- **One source of truth** — the same report is visible in the mobile app, the web app, the customer portal and the PDF export.
- **Photo and signature native** — no separate app, no email attachments, no paper to scan.
- **Pattern detection** — the recent issues feed surfaces problems before customers do.
- **Multilingual** — every worker uses the report in their preferred language, so quality data is consistent across multilingual teams.
